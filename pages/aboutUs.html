About Us

CertAdgeHub is a project by CodeHub PH, a platform that allows users to store and share their certifications and badges.

With CertadgeHub, users can easily upload and manage their certification documents, and can even embed their certificates on other websites or social media platforms.

Partners can also use CertadgeHub to issue their own certificates, adding their own branding and information to the certificates. CertadgeHub is a convenient and secure way to showcase your achievements and build your professional profile.

Developed by the talented team at CodeHub PH, CertAdgeHub is the premier destination for managing and sharing your professional accomplishments.

CodeHub Philippines is a community of skilled professionals and students who are passionate about technology and coding. At CodeHub Philippines, we believe in sharing our knowledge and expertise with the next generation of developers. Our team is dedicated to mentoring students and helping them learn the latest programming languages and technologies. We strive to create a welcoming and supportive environment where people of all skill levels can come together to learn, collaborate, and grow as developers. Whether you're just starting out on your coding journey or are a seasoned pro, CodeHub Philippines is the perfect place to connect with like-minded individuals and further your knowledge and skills.
